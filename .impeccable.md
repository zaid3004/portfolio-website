# Project Design Context: Zaid Shaheed Portfolio

## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: Internship providers and technical recruiters at high-tier firms.
- **Secondary**: Potential collaborators and the developer community.
- **Context**: Users are evaluating Zaid's technical depth, professional readiness, and attention to detail.

### Brand Personality
- **Blueprint-like**: Structured, precise, and foundational.
- **Intentional**: Every element (spacing, typography, grid) serves a purpose.
- **Sophisticated**: Refined and academic, avoiding the "loud" patterns of modern SaaS or the "neon" aesthetics of gaming.
- **3-Word Personality**: **Structured, Impeccable, Articulate.**

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Clean architectural blueprint meets editorial notebook. High-contrast serif headers paired with technical mono-spaced labels.
- **Theme**: Warm parchment backgrounds, deep ink-blue (#2b4162) and brass/leather accents (#b08968).
- **Anti-references**: No generic "SaaS" landing pages, no "Gamer" neons, no marketplace/template "basic" layouts.

### Design Principles
1. **Grid as Foundation**: Use visible or implied grids (dot-grids, subtle lines) to reinforce the "blueprint" structure.
2. **Typographic Hierarchy**: High contrast between serif (display) and mono (utility) fonts to signify the balance between "vision" and "implementation."
3. **Intentional White Space**: Allow the content to breathe; avoid the "cramped" feel of generic templates.
4. **Data-First Modals**: Maintain the logic where every "card" is a deep-dive into structured data, reinforcing the "blueprints" for each project.
